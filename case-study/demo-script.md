# SalesSphere Demo Video Script

**Target Length:** 60 - 90 Seconds
**Format:** 1080p or 4K, 16:9
**Music:** Ambient, calm electronic (e.g. Tycho style)

## Section 1: The Hook (0:00 - 0:15)

**Visual:**
- Start on a sleek black screen.
- Fade in the logo: "SalesSphere."
- Fade out, and transition smoothly into the `Overview` page using a slow zoom.
- The `Overview` page's Framer Motion entrance animations play (Header dropping in, cards fading up).
- Focus in on the "Executive Brief" section.

**Voiceover/Text Overlay:**
"Enterprise software shouldn't just show data. It should explain the business."
"Welcome to SalesSphere."

## Section 2: Context Before Visualization (0:15 - 0:35)

**Visual:**
- Mouse smoothly moves to hover over the Metric Cards. Show the hover state (slight lift up, border glow, sparkline drawing in).
- Click on the `Performance` tab in the navigation.
- Smooth transition. Show the "Revenue Momentum" chart. 
- Hover over the chart. Ensure the custom tooltip (with the blur effect and custom styling) is clearly visible. Show the crosshair interacting with the data.

**Voiceover/Text Overlay:**
"Move beyond raw metrics."
"Contextual insights that tell the story behind the numbers."

## Section 3: Explainable Intelligence (0:35 - 0:55)

**Visual:**
- Click on the `Intelligence` tab.
- Pan over the "Executive Recommendations" panel.
- Highlight the "Confidence", "Impact", and the "Explainability Panel" (Why?).
- Mouse hovers over the "Approve & Assign" button.

**Voiceover/Text Overlay:**
"Decisions powered by Machine Intelligence."
"With full explainability, so you always know *why*."

## Section 4: Premium Experience (0:55 - 1:15)

**Visual:**
- Fast-paced, rhythmic cuts synced to the music.
- Cut 1: `Products` page, hovering over the Donut Chart (margins).
- Cut 2: `Customers` page, hovering over High Priority churn risk.
- Cut 3: The Profile dropdown opening smoothly.
- Cut 4: The compare dropdown in the top bar selecting "Previous Quarter".

**Voiceover/Text Overlay:**
"A meticulously crafted experience."
"Built on an 8pt design system."
"Pixels that build trust."

## Section 5: The Call to Action (1:15 - 1:30)

**Visual:**
- Slow pan out of the dashboard.
- Fade to black.
- Final text: "SalesSphere. Calm Enterprise Intelligence."
- Subtext: "View the source on GitHub."

**Voiceover/Text Overlay:**
"SalesSphere."
"Calm Enterprise Intelligence."
